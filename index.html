The Frontmatters Story

Frontmatters is the leader in the movement to teach the Nigerian Youths technical sophistication, which includes both "hard skills" like coding, command lines, and version control and "soft skills" like guessing keyboard shortcuts, Googling error messages and knowing when to just reboot the darn thing.

We believe there are at least a million people who can benefit from learning technical sophistication, probably more. To join our movement, sign up for our official email list now.

Background

Frontmatters is an outgrowth of many years of self-taught technical sophistication experience by its founder - St. Asuzu Alfred P. M. This site is one of the sample site he developed during his quest for technical sophistication on CSS and Layout.

WHY THIS PROGRAMME IS FOR YOU

Frontmatters was founded in 2018 by St. Asuzu Alfred P. M.. He believes that the kind of technical sophistication taught by the Frontmatters can benefit at least two million Nigerian youths and probably more.

This programme is driven by his desire to see every young school leaver between ages 15 and 25 who is financially challenged with furthering his/her education, attain self reliance with Web and Software Development.

Frontmatters will nuture you through its well and carefully selected guarnteed courses to the freedom to organize your own life, make your own decisions without needing much help from other people.

We value your independence!

Beginning Development

When you are done with Web Basics and Developer Fundamentals, you would be learning the foundations of developing web applications. We shall start by learning to manipulate the page with JavaScript to do cool stuffs (mind-blowing stuffs). Then, we'll focus on the Ruby language. We shall start with the Ruby language, and then, will cover Sinatra, a simple Ruby web framework, to get you ready to dive into the Ruby on Rails tutorial.
